The Notre Dame English Club, one of the most dynamic platforms of Notre Dame College, has long been dedicated to enriching students with language, confidence, and creativity. It started its journey with the motto

WHOLE UNIVERSE IN ONE SOUL
LEARN ENGLISH TO REACH THE GOAL

Since its founding in 2005 as the 19th club of Notre Dame College, the English Club has grown into one of the largest and most active communities on campus. Over the years, it has provided countless opportunities for students to explore language, develop creativity, and build confidence. By encouraging critical thinking, leadership, and self-expression, the club continues to shape generations of students while staying true to its guiding vision.

Activities

Exciting Events

Our events combine creativity, collaboration, and spirited competition. From club activities to national-level challenges, participants showcase talents, push boundaries, and engage in experiences that foster teamwork, achievement, and a vibrant community spirit.

Insightful Sessions

Our sessions provide a space for curiosity and growth. Members explore new ideas, develop language and communication skills, and engage in activities that strengthen critical thinking. Each session is designed to inspire learning while fostering confidence and a love for English.

Competitive Quizzing

NDEC’s quiz teams engage in a variety of competitions and events that challenge knowledge and teamwork. Members sharpen their skills, test their quick thinking, and experience the excitement of friendly competition both within the club and beyond.
